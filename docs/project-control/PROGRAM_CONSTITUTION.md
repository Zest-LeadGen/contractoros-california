# ContractorOS Program Constitution

## Authority And Purpose — Documentation Scope

This is the top-level public-safe authority for ContractorOS program direction. It records approved direction, not implemented capability. GitHub issues, protected pull requests, exact-SHA external review, human/write-access approval, branch protection, merge verification, and issue closeout remain mandatory phase gates. No provision in this Constitution independently authorizes implementation.

Precedence is: applicable law and binding obligations; explicit later owner amendments recorded in durable GitHub evidence; this Constitution and the Owner Decision Register; approved ADRs and control policies; phase issues and reports; implementation documentation. A later record supersedes an earlier record only when it names the superseded record and preserves audit history.

## Three-Horizon Program

1. Horizon 1: governed California validation, beginning with Law & Business and preserving C-10 as a priority follow-on condition.
2. Horizon 2: validated California platform expansion and one evidence-selected contrasting pilot state.
3. Horizon 3: evidence-driven regional and national expansion through reusable jurisdiction packs and local overlays.

The twenty-four-month California full-scale platform objective is a tracked program target, not an unconditional readiness, release, revenue, or completion guarantee. Evidence gates may change timing or scope.

## Automation And Human Control

The 95% automation objective means automation of eligible routine workflow steps only. In this documentation scope, high-impact legal, financial, security, privacy, public-release, production, architecture, approval, merge, budget, credential, and policy decisions retain protected human authority. Independent red-team review, exact-SHA binding, current human/write-access approval, and branch protection remain controlling.

## Product And Market Priority

California remains first. Law & Business is the first governed learning and content priority. C-10 remains a priority follow-on after its source, originality, legal-currentness, evaluation, and release gates are authorized and satisfied. Design-partner interviews, workflow observation, prototypes, and paid validation are approved future work only; pricing remains experimental until willingness-to-pay and retention evidence exists.

Answer positions in future test-prep experiences must be randomized or balanced deterministically without changing correctness, explanation linkage, analytics identity, or accessibility. Public question work and lawful source ingestion or database construction require separate later gates.

## Architecture Direction

ContractorOS uses a modular-monolith-first direction with explicit module boundaries. Service extraction requires measured need. National architecture uses ContractorOS Core, a Federal Base Pack, State Packs, County/City/AHJ Overlays, Licensing-Body Overlays, and Utility/Service-Territory Overlays.

## Parallel Work And Canonical State

No more than three non-overlapping active pull requests may be considered. Any shared canonical-state file is serialized: only one active pull request may change it at a time, and later work must rebase its evidence through the protected lifecycle rather than assuming unmerged state.

## Public And Private Evidence

The public repository may contain public-safe code, schemas, sanitized state, ADRs, policy, citations, and release evidence. Private owner documents, strategy, confidential legal review, customer information, secrets, private AI traces, incident material, sensitive budgets, vendor discussions, original ZIP/DOCX files, and proprietary source packages are not public repository artifacts. Public records may reference private evidence only by approved opaque identifier or content hash.

## Supersession And History

Historical decisions are append-only evidence. Corrections add explicit supersession links; they do not silently rewrite history. Obsolete labels such as 4K-7A through 4K-7E do not overwrite repository phase history. Any valid concepts from those labels may be remapped only after Phase 4K-12 through a later gate.

## Current Gate Boundary

Issue #47 implements documentation, schemas, ADRs, and source-of-truth reconciliation only. The continuity collector, startup-packet generator, private control plane, legal-intelligence system, product modules, agents, credentials, paid services, Phase 4K-9, and Phase 4I are not implemented or authorized by this gate.
