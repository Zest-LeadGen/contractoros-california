# ADR-008 — Product Validation, Monetization, And Parallel Work

## Status
Approved future-work direction; research and monetization are not started.

## Related Owner Decision IDs
D22, D23, D24.

## Context
Market learning can run alongside governance only when data, spending, and canonical-state conflicts are controlled.

## Decision
Permit future design-partner interviews, workflow observation, prototypes, and paid validation under dedicated gates. Pricing remains experimental. Limit active PRs to three non-overlapping lanes and serialize canonical-state changes.

## Alternatives Considered
Price-first launch; unlimited parallel PRs; no market research until product completion.

## Rejected Alternatives
They create unsupported claims, merge conflicts, or delayed evidence.

## Consequences
Research may proceed only after consent, spending, and data controls are approved.

## Risks
Customer-data exposure, biased evidence, premature monetization, and state conflicts.

## Controls
Research plan, consent, evidence quality, budget approval, overlap checks, and serialized files.

## Implementation Prerequisites
Market-validation gate and parallel-lane control.

## Non-Authorized Scope
No interviews, prototype, payment experiment, customer-data use, or pricing launch.

## Validation Requirements
Willingness-to-pay, retention, research quality, and PR-overlap evidence.

## Supersession Links
No existing product phase is resumed or superseded.
